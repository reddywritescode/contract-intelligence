and

CloudBridge Solutions LLC, a Texas limited liability company ("Vendor")

1. PROJECT OVERVIEW

1.1 Project Name: Electronic Health Records (EHR) Cloud Migration & Modernization

1.2 Objective: Vendor shall migrate Client's on-premises EHR system (approximately 2.4 million patient records) to a cloud-native architecture on AWS, ensuring HIPAA compliance, zero data loss, and maximum 4 hours of planned downtime during cutover.

2. SCOPE OF WORK

2.1 Phase 1 — Assessment & Planning (Weeks 1–4)
- Conduct infrastructure audit of existing on-premises environment
- Map all data dependencies and integration points
- Develop detailed migration plan and risk assessment
- Deliver Architecture Design Document (ADD) for Client approval

2.2 Phase 2 — Development & Testing (Weeks 5–16)
- Build cloud infrastructure on AWS (VPC, EKS, RDS, S3)
- Develop data migration scripts and ETL pipelines
- Implement HIPAA-compliant encryption (AES-256 at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit)
- Conduct parallel testing with production data subset (anonymized)
- Perform load testing to validate performance under 3x projected peak load

2.3 Phase 3 — Migration & Cutover (Weeks 17–20)
- Execute staged migration with rollback capability
- Cutover production traffic during approved maintenance window
- Monitor system stability for 72-hour hypercare period
- Decommission legacy infrastructure upon Client sign-off

2.4 Phase 4 — Post-Migration Support (Weeks 21–32)
- Provide 90-day post-migration support with 24/7 on-call engineering
- Conduct performance optimization and cost right-sizing
- Train Client's IT team (minimum 20 staff) on cloud operations
- Deliver final documentation and knowledge transfer
